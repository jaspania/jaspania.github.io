Jaspanian Territories

Here is a list of all territories within Jaspania. Each territory has extremely different job opportunities and purposes.

The Isle of Jaspania

Most of the main Jaspanian Isle has a subtropical or tropical climate, with many tipes of farming as the main business. Particularly tropical parts also serve as tourist destinations.

The Capital

Right in the center of the Jaspanian Island lies the Jaspanian capital. In the Jaspanian capital you will find wildly different opportunities to the rest of the Island, as there are bustling businesses and large urban centers. Here you can get a higher eductation and enter a skilled trade.

Jaspanian Siberia

Recently, Russia kindly ceded control of part of Siberia over to Jaspania. Jaspania has rapidly industrialised this portion, and it now serves as the economic heart of Jaspania, with many factories, mines, and cargo ports to engage with trade with the rest of the world.

Fr*nce

After the Russo-Jaspo-Fr*nch war, Jaspania gained control of the entirety of France. The whole area now serves as a prison for Jaspania's worst criminals.
